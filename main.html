Pac-man

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move.

One key press moves you once.

Collect all of the points in the level to progress to the next level.

Don't let the ghost catch you.

Press the start button to start.

Press the reset button to reset the level and the hit the start button to start.

The small dots are worth 10 points and the big dots are worth 50 points.

Collect 2600 points to win.

Good luck.
